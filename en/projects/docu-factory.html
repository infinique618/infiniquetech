Project 06 / AI & Software

Docu-Factory.

Evidence-based AI document generation platform

AISoftware

핵심 기술

Evidence-anchored architecture, claim/counter-claim generation, LLM orchestration, document indexing.

프로젝트 설명

An AI orchestration pipeline that automates evidence-based document generation. Source collection, structural indexing, evidence tracing, and claim-graph design are integrated end-to-end. Every sentence links back to its source so evidence is traceable. Claims and counter-claims are structured so the output is logical document production, not mere generation. Quality gates and an API-based orchestrator lock in reliability and reproducibility.
